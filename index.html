ColorWise Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: ColorWise is designed with privacy-first principles. We believe in transparency and giving you complete control over your data.

Data Collection and Usage

ColorWise does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All image processing and color detection happens locally on your device. We do not have access to your camera images, and no data is sent to external servers.

Camera Permission

ColorWise requests camera permission to provide real-time color detection functionality. Camera access is used solely for:

  • Real-time color identification
  • Image capture for color analysis
  • No images are stored or transmitted without your explicit action

Storage Permission

Storage permission is requested only when you choose to save processed images. This permission allows you to:

  • Save color-corrected images to your device
  • Access your photo gallery for image upload
  • All saves are user-initiated and stored locally

Voice Feedback

Voice feedback uses your device's built-in text-to-speech system. No voice data is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Machine Learning

All color detection and correction happens on-device using TensorFlow Lite models. No data is sent to external services for processing.

Third-Party Services

ColorWise does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.

Contact Us

Summary: ColorWise respects your privacy. We don't collect your data, everything works offline, and you have full control over your information.
